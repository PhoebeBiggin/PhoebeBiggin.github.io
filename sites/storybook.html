Prep - Grade 2 Induction Storybook

As part of a promotional package to onboard early primary school-aged (Prep - year 2) student families to the Methodist Ladies College Primary school sector, MLC’s Community Engagement department commissioned a short-form illustrated storybook titled ‘Welcome to MLC’. Telling a story about starting at MLC for the first time, The Welcome book presents the opportunities and unique experiences that await students in an encouraging, optimistic perspective to build excitement amongst incoming students and parents about starting at MLC and make them feel part of the MLC community. The Storybook to reflect the children who come to MLC, from their diverse walks of life, so that students can truly relate to the story and feel ready for their next step in education.
