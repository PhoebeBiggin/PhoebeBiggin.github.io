Silver & Green 2022 - 2023

The "Silver and Green" is the official student magazine of Methodist Ladies College. Capturing school life, achievements, and student contributions, with archives going back to the early 20th century. Each year of the magazine aims to symbolise the year 7-12 Cohort’s identity, presenting opportunities, events, activities and articles. Each release of the Silver and Green (produced by an editorial team over 10 months) is presented as memorabilia to the student community. Over the 2022-2023 Editions, the magazine’s art direction incorporates the themes of ‘campus & community’ and illustrative pieces to attract its predominantly female audience.
