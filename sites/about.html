Hi! I'm Phoebe Biggin: A melbournian Graphic, UX & Product Designer - I draw, I design, I create: I love all things creative.

My work holds a resolution around understanding and improving the personal, human point of interaction with graphic and digital media – I focus my work to play and engage the perspective of a ‘user’, albeit through the act of viewing, using and understanding. I have worked within art direction, illustration and web design, and UX research and production. I am currently pursuing an education in both Graphic Design and User Experience fields, alongside a specialisation in Marketing, to provide a multi-point perspective in creating products that resonate with their users. Within my approach to design, I aim to excite, engage, and incorporate the user as the focal point of my practice.
