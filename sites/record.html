Record Branding Collection

Based on the eccentric, upbeat, 80’s disco music produced by the band, Wooze. This project encourages its viewers to examine the detail and ‘explore’ the scenes across the cover, posters and branding alike, the storytelling album. Presenting a debut record packaging design, poster series and additional promotional media, this project aims to provide a playful and youthful atmosphere to align with the band’s modern abstract themes.
