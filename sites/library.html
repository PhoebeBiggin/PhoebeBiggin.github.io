Fiction Library Media series

In collaboration with the Librarian team at Walton Library (located at Methodist Ladies College), a series of 36 poster designs was commissioned to represent the range of fiction genres in the reading space. This project embraces MLC’s core motto of being ‘World ready’ and reflects the diverse community of students and educators through 18 themed character designs to be used within the library complex. Each poster has emphasised its own inspirational roots in the student body of Methodist Ladies College, past and present.
