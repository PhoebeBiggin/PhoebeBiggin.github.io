Methodist Ladies College Swim School Mural

MLC Aquatic is an MLC is a multi-age swim school, ranging from with lessons available from 3 years of age to competitive swimming programs run in partnership with the MLC Aquatic Swim Club. The Methodist Ladies College Sport Department and MLC Swimming sought to commission a large student-focused mural at the eastern end of the pool, bringing to life what swimming at MLC means for students. The Mural project offers a vibrant, friendly, and community-focused representation of the diverse range of swim school activities for students.
